2. Step-ups (45s) - 12"/BW/15 reps

   - Cues: "Drive through heel", "Control descent"

3. TRX Rows (45s) - 45°/12 reps
   - Purpose: Postural strength
   - Cues: "Squeeze shoulder blades"

   - Regression: Upright stance

4. Dead Bug (45s) - 20 reps
   - Purpose: Core engagement
   - Cues: "Press low back into mat"
   - Progression: Add extensions
   - Regression: Bent knees
